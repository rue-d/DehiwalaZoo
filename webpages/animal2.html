More Information on Turtles

The loggerhead sea turtle (Caretta caretta), is a species of oceanic turtle distributed throughout the world. It is a marine reptile, belonging to the family Cheloniidae. The average loggerhead measures around 90 cm (35 in) in carapace length when fully grown. Adults have an approximate weight range of 80 to 200 kg (180 to 440 lb), averaging around 135 kg (298 lb), and a straight-line length range of 70 to 95 cm (28 to 37 in) The turtle's skin ranges from yellow to brown in color, and the shell is typically reddish brown.
